Instagram Copyright and Content Policy (Extracted)

1. Only the copyright owner or their authorized representative may submit a copyright infringement claim.
2. Instagram removes content that infringes copyright under its Community Guidelines and Terms of Use.
3. Claims must clearly identify both the original copyrighted work and the allegedly infringing content, including links, usernames, and descriptions.
4. Claims must include a request for removal of the infringing content and a statement of ownership.
5. Instagram may share the claimant’s contact details with the alleged infringer.
6. The claim should be polite, specific, and reference Instagram’s official reporting process.
7. The email must be in English, formal, and persuasive, suitable for submission to Instagram’s copyright team.
8. Reference: Instagram’s Community Guidelines (https://help.instagram.com/477434105621119) and Terms of Use (https://help.instagram.com/478745558852511)

Excerpt from Instagram Community Guidelines:
"Instagram is committed to helping people and organizations protect their intellectual property rights. The Community Guidelines prohibit posting content that violates someone else’s intellectual property rights, including copyright. If you repeatedly post content that infringes others’ intellectual property rights, your account may be disabled or removed."

Excerpt from Instagram Copyright FAQs:
"If you believe something on Instagram infringes your copyright, you can report it by using Instagram’s copyright complaint form. Only the copyright owner or their authorized representative may submit a report of copyright infringement. When you submit a copyright complaint, Instagram may forward the information you provide (including your contact info) to the person who posted the allegedly infringing content."

Excerpt from Instagram’s Copyright Complaint Form:
"Please provide:
- A description of your copyrighted work
- A description of the content you believe infringes your copyright
- The location (URL) of the infringing content
- Your contact information
- A statement that you believe in good faith that the use is not authorized
- A statement that the information is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner"
